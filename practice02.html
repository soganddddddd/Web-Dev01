sogand recepie for hot chocolate


ingredients :

  • 2 cups of milk (any kind you prefer, such as whole, almond, or soy)
  • 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows (optional, for topping)

instruction :

  1. Heat the Milk: In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it's warm but not boiling.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a separate small bowl, mix the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt.
  3. Combine: Add the cocoa mixture to the warm milk, whisking continuously to combine. Continue heating and whisking until the mixture is hot and all the cocoa and sugar have dissolved.
  4. Add Vanilla: Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Serve: Pour the hot chocolate into a mug. Top with whipped cream or marshmallows if desired.

tips :

  • For a richer hot chocolate, you can add a piece of dark chocolate or use half-and-half instead of milk.
  • Experiment with flavors by adding a dash of cinnamon, nutmeg, or peppermint extract.
